Whisk It Shoppe Site

For my Digital Information Design senior capstone course, my group was challenged to create an online presence for a Brick and Mortar Bakery in Rock Hill. the owner desired for her costumers to be able to order custom and all sorts of sweet treats online. I contributed mostly on the backend portion of the site, creating and efficient system for catering orders to be stored as well as account information for both admin and customers using Google Firebase and Firestore.
